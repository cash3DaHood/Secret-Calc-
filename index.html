How it works

Chance = Unit Base × (1 + Secret Chance) × Luck Multiplier. Luck uses a breakpoint at 14 − Star Luck: below it, luck scales ×0.5; above it, ×0.05 (the soft cap). Average time uses your open speed from the panel. Fan-made tool — values mirror the original community spreadsheet and may differ slightly from in-game rounding.
